Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Branded. ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Contact details (name, email address)
  • Business information relevant to your project
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment provider)
  • Project requirements and specifications
  • Communications with our team

Information collected automatically:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and general location
  • Pages visited and interactions with our site
  • Referring website

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver the services you request
  • Communicate about your project
  • Process payments
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send relevant updates (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Service providers who assist our operations (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Delivered Agent

The AI agents we build for you are designed to run locally in your environment. Once delivered:

  • You control all data processed by your agent
  • Your agent does not send data back to us
  • You are responsible for the security of your agent and its data

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and analyze site usage. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing the information we hold about you
  • Correcting inaccurate information
  • Requesting deletion of your information
  • Opting out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@brandedjustlikethat.com

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Project files may be retained for a reasonable period to support refinement requests and reference.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at hello@brandedjustlikethat.com
